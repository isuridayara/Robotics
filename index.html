Introduction to Robotics

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that combines computer science, electronics, mechanical engineering, and artificial intelligence to design and build intelligent machines. Robots are used in many industries including manufacturing, healthcare, space exploration, and education.

Robotics systems typically include sensors (to collect data), controllers (such as microcontrollers or computers), actuators (motors and servos), and software that enables decision-making. With the advancement of AI and automation, robotics continues to grow rapidly and plays a vital role in modern technology.
